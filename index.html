FAQs
Frequently Asked questions

What platforms is Headbangers Rhythm Royale ?

Headbangers will be available on Xbox Series S I X, Xbox One, PS5, PS4, Steam, Windows Store and Nintendo Switch.

What is Headbangers Rhythm Royale ?

Well, think no more! Headbangers puts you and 29 others into the eye of the Pigeon while you battle it out in rhythmic challenges to find out who is the ultimate Master Headbanger.

What is the Season Pass / Pigeon Pass ?

The ‘Pigeon Pass’ grants players access to three unique DLC packs. The first DLC pack available, ‘Flock & Roll’, features four full costumes, four garments, a taunt and five taunt voices, and one neck sound, with content for the two remaining DLC packs being available before April 2024.

Will there be updates after release ?

Absolutely! Keep your pigeon eyes peeled on our social media accounts as we will be sharing more information about Season 2 and Season 3 in the upcoming months.

How many minigames does Headbangers have ?

Headbangers contains 23 minigames that all test different skills, including your rhythm, accuracy, memory, reaction speed and even your ability to rap!
